It is an element on the bottom of screen to show actions by multiselect of some items. It works with custom content.

  1. Add TuiActionsBarsHostModule into your app.module

  2. Add tui-actions-bars-host into your app.template

  3. Use tuiActionsBar directive to open table bar

  4. Alternatively, you can user TuiActionsBarService. Use open method and subscribe to returned Observable
